I am Santhosh. My mom Lakshmi took full responsibility for my growth and development. She is everything to me. I regard her as a substitute for a father, brother, sister or friend because she has spent all her love and time to bring me up. She has toiled day & night from my childhood and continued to take care of me until now. I am proud of my mother and love her above anything else in this world. Because she has spent every drop of her blood and sweat for her only child - that is me! She has comforted me and refused nothing that would make me happy. She is my queen, and I am her prince. She has made such a sacrifice for my sake, and I owe my life, success, and happiness to her. In this world, she is my only treasure. I adore her and want to prove that I am her most grateful son.

I did my basic schooling at ASHOKA NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL, MADURAI from my KG's to III standard. Then I continued until the seventh standard at FES MATRICULATION SCHOOL, MADURAI. I joined V.H.N. HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL, MADURAI and completed my higher secondary. I completed my BE in ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING from PANNAI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY, SIVAGANGAI. I was a JRF at Indian Institute of Technology - Hyderabad. I did my MTech from IIT Hyderabad. Presently I am pursuing my Ph.D. from IIT Hyd.
